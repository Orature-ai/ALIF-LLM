The ALIF الف Model Series

A Family of Urdu Generative Models for Diverse Applications

We have developed a series of generative language models, collectively named ALIF الف, specifically pretrained on our large-scale ALIF-Urdu-Corpus. Our primary focus has been on creating computationally efficient Small Language Models (SLMs) that offer a strong balance between performance and the resources required for training and deployment. The series includes both base pretrained models and instruction-tuned variants.
