Data Curation: The Foundation of ALIF

Building a Comprehensive Urdu Corpus

High-quality, large-scale data is crucial for building effective language models. Recognizing the scarcity of comprehensive Urdu datasets suitable for pretraining, we undertook a significant data curation effort. This involved scraping diverse sources, implementing a robust cleaning and deduplication pipeline, and ensuring cultural relevance. Our goal was to create a corpus that not only captures the breadth of the Urdu language but also its nuances, making it an ideal foundation for training our ALIF الف models.
