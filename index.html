ALIF الف: Building efficient and culturally-aware Urdu language models from the ground up.

Abstract

This project introduces a computationally efficient Small Language Model (SLM) for Urdu, aiming to establish a scalable framework for underrepresented languages. While existing large multilingual models like GPT, LLaMA, and DeepSeek excel in high-resource languages, they often overlook the linguistic and cultural nuances of Urdu due to limited representation in training data. Their high deployment costs also pose barriers for local institutions. Our approach addresses these gaps by curating a large, culturally informed Urdu dataset and developing an SLM that rivals LLMs in performance while remaining lightweight and accessible. Emphasizing sustainability and task-specific design, this work challenges the one-size-fits-all LLM paradigm and promotes AI accessibility, data sovereignty, and equitable technological development—paving the way for similar efforts in other low-resource languages.
